Sun Safety: Protect Your Skin

Embrace the sunshine responsibly with these essential tips!

Essential Sun Safety Tips

Sunscreen is Your Best Friend

Apply broad-spectrum SPF 30 sunscreen generously to all exposed skin 15 minutes before sun exposure. Reapply every two hours, or more often if swimming or sweating.

Shield Your Eyes

Wear sunglasses that block UVA and UVB rays. Look for a label that says "UV 400" or "100% UV protection."

Seek Shade During Peak Hours

The sun's rays are strongest between 10 am and 4 pm. Limit direct sun exposure during this time and seek shade whenever possible.

Cover Up When Needed

Wear protective clothing, such as long-sleeved shirts, pants, and wide-brimmed hats, for extra sun protection.
